=============================
PLUGIN DESCRIPTION
=============================
Implementing AddThis social sharing buttons on your Elgg website.

This Plugin uses AddThis sharing tools to implement third-party buttons like Google+1, Twitt and Facebook Like.

AddThis is the global leader for sharing and social insights.
Learn more about AddThis: http://www.addthis.com/

Features:
Complete share buttons (more than 300 bookmark and share buttons available)
3 styles of button groups to choose
Adjust to display left aligned or right aligned

=============================
REQUIREMENT(S)
=============================
Elgg 1.8.3+
AddThis Profile ID

=============================
INSTRALLATION
=============================
Copy the unzipped addthis_share folder to the "mod" directory in your Elgg installation.

You must then activate the plugin:

    Log in to your Elgg site with your administrator account
    Go to Administration -> Configure -> Plugins
    Find your plugin in the list of installed plugins and click on the 'Activate' button.
    
In the plugin's setting, enter your AddThis Profile ID, choose alignment and button styles, then save.

=============================
UPGRADING
=============================

1. Deactivate the Plugin
2. Overwrite your existing Plugin files
3. Go to Plugin's setting and enter your AddThis Profile ID, choose alignment and button styles, then save.
